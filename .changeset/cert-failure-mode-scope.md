Add failure-mode competency scoping document for the certification program.

Maps 12 failure modes (the 9 from #2386 plus 3 additions from expert review)
to certification modules, depth targets, and assessment approaches. Authoring
follow-ups per module are deferred per the issue's "Done when" criteria.

Includes a reclassification of TMP attestation failure (marked
experimental/future in AdCP 3.0) to TMP provider integration failure
(adagents.json binding, seller_agent mismatch, bypass mode) — the real
operational failure mode in current protocol.

Refs #2386.
