Teach the triage routine to bundle ready items from a multi-item issue into one PR rather than splitting the issue into peer child issues. When a maintainer files a follow-up list, the bot opens one cohesive PR for everything that's ready and leaves the parent issue open with a comment about what shipped vs. what remains. Issue-to-issue splits only happen via explicit human "convert to epic" escalation — never autonomously. Reduces GitHub-click proliferation and keeps PR reviews cohesive.
