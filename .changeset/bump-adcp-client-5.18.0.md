Bump `@adcp/client` from 5.17.0 → 5.18.0. The 5.18.0 release adds two new storyboard validators (`a2a_context_continuity` for cross-step A2A `contextId` correlation, `a2a_submitted_artifact` for A2A `submitted`-arm wire-shape conformance), fixes the `SubmittedContinuation.taskId` plumbing so polls address the server-assigned task handle, aligns `tasks/get` polling with adcp#3126's typed `result` field, and ships a `typecheck:skill-examples` ratchet plus a matrix-conformance harness that caught real drift in 8 SKILL.md files. Adapts our `renderAllHintFixPlans` helper in `storyboard-fix-plan.ts` to accept the broader `StoryboardStepHint` union (5 variants in 5.18.0) by filtering on `kind === 'context_value_rejected'` — the formatter only renders that variant; other hint kinds (`shape_drift`, `missing_required_field`, `format_mismatch`, `monotonic_violation`) are diagnostic-only and don't have a prescribed fix-plan format yet (tracking generalised rendering at adcp-client#935).
