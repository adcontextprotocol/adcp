Add collection lists — program-level brand safety for shows, series, and other content programs across platforms. Collection lists are a parallel construct to property lists: they use distribution identifiers (IMDb, Gracenote, EIDR) for cross-publisher matching and support content rating and genre filters. New targeting overlay fields (collection_list, collection_list_exclude) enable both inclusion and exclusion. New genre taxonomy enum normalizes genre classification across buyers and sellers.
