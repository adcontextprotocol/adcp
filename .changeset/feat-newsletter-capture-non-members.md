Add lightweight newsletter capture for non-members. Anonymous visitors can now subscribe from the site footer or an inline card at the end of Stories. Submitting an email writes a pending confirmation keyed by that email (no WorkOS user yet) and sends a branded confirmation email from `hello@updates.agenticadvertising.org`. Clicking the single-use 24-hour token provisions a lightweight WorkOS user (`emailVerified: false`), flips `marketing_opt_in` to true, and lands the visitor on `/welcome-subscribed.html`. Confirm does not create a session; full account access still requires OAuth. Preserves the invariant that every subscriber is a first-class WorkOS account, and gates account creation behind proof-of-inbox so we don't grow unconfirmed accounts. IP rate-limited (5 subscribes/min, 30 confirms/min) and per-email cooldown (one confirmation email per inbox per 10 min).
