Addie: ground company-specific statements in registry data only

When Addie retrieves a member's registry profile, she now treats it as the authoritative source for company-specific statements and will not supplement it with training-data assumptions about the company's products or capabilities. If the profile is sparse, she falls back to generic role-based context ("as a sell-side member…"). If no profile exists, she makes no company-specific claims at all. Fixes a bug where Addie projected unregistered product capabilities onto a member company during certification teaching.
