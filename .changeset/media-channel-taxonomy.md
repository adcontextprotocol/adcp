Add Media Channel Taxonomy specification with standardized channel definitions.

- Introduces 19 media channels (web, ctv, mobile_app, social, search, linear_tv, radio, streaming_audio, podcast, dooh, ooh, print, cinema, email, gaming, influencer, conversational, sponsorship, commerce_media)
- Clear distinction between channels (where ads appear), formats (how ads render), and buying models (how inventory is purchased)
- Updates channels.json enum with new taxonomy values
- Adds enumDescriptions to channels.json and property-type.json
- Includes migration guide from legacy channel values (display, video, audio, native, retail)
