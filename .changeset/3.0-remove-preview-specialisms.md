Remove four preview specialisms (`sales-streaming-tv`, `sales-exchange`, `sales-retail-media`, `measurement-verification`) from the 3.0 GA taxonomy. They were causing confusion about what's stable vs preview at GA. Sellers in those categories should claim `sales-guaranteed` or `sales-non-guaranteed` at 3.0; re-introduction with authoritative storyboards is tracked for 3.1 in adcontextprotocol/adcp#2511.

Also fills the `brand` protocol baseline storyboard (identity-only — rights lifecycle remains in the experimental `brand-rights` specialism), threads the 3.0 trust primitives (idempotency, RFC 9421 request signing, signed webhooks, governance JWS) through the quickstart and intro walkthrough, and cleans up cross-references in the compliance catalog, specialist learning modules, migration guide, and what's new page.
