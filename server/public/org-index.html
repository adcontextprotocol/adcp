What is agentic advertising?

Agentic advertising is a shift in how advertising works. Instead of manually managing campaigns across dozens of platforms, AI agents handle the execution — discovering inventory, pushing your brand data to platforms, placing buys, and optimizing toward your goals. You set the strategy and approve what matters.

For brands and agencies

Your job shifts from setting up campaigns on every platform to providing the right ingredients — product catalogs, brand guidelines, content standards — and defining what success looks like. Agents do the rest across every platform through one standard.

For platforms

Agents are allocating media budgets. If your platform speaks the standard, any buyer agent can discover your inventory and transact — without custom integrations for each one.

Why it matters now

New media channels — AI assistants, AI search, generative experiences — need new approaches. You can't place an IO or send a bid request for AI-generated advertising. Open standards make it work at scale.

Ad Context Protocol (AdCP)

The open standard enabling AI agents to execute advertising tasks across any platform. From media buying to creative generation to audience activation.

adagents.json

A standard discovery mechanism for advertising agents. Add an adagents.json file to your domain to make your agents discoverable by AI assistants and orchestrators.

brand.json

A discovery mechanism for brand identity. Host a brand.json file on your domain to claim your brand, declare your portfolio, and make your identity verifiable by AI agents.
