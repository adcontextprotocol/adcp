What is Agentic Advertising?

Agentic advertising is a new paradigm where AI agents autonomously execute advertising tasks on behalf of brands, agencies, and publishers. Instead of humans manually navigating dozens of platforms, configuring campaigns, and reconciling data, AI agents handle these operations through standardized protocols.

This isn't just automation. It's a fundamental shift in how advertising gets done:

  • Natural language briefs replace complex platform interfaces
  • Agents negotiate inventory, pricing, and placement across publishers
  • Open standards enable interoperability between any buyer and seller
  • Humans focus on strategy while agents handle execution

Why Now?

The advertising ecosystem has been fragmented for decades. Every platform has its own API, workflow, and data format. AI agents can bridge this fragmentation, but only if they speak a common language. That's where open standards come in.

Learn more about the broader agentic protocol landscape - including how horizontal middleware like the Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF) works alongside vertical protocols like AdCP and the Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP).

AAO vs. AdCP

Key distinction: AdCP is a technical standard. AgenticAdvertising.org is the organization ensuring standards like AdCP develop as shared infrastructure that benefits everyone.

About AgenticAdvertising.org

Our Mission

Rebuild the media and marketing ecosystem around collaboration. After decades of fragmentation that separated creativity from distribution and buried strategy under infrastructure challenges, AI is enabling something fundamentally different: open standards, transparent practices, and interoperable systems that let brands, agencies, and publishers focus on what actually drives business results.

AgenticAdvertising.org exists to make sure this opportunity doesn't get lost to proprietary platforms and fragmented approaches. We're an independent industry organization dedicated to ensuring agentic AI develops as shared infrastructure that benefits the entire ecosystem.

What We Do

  • Standards Development - Govern and evolve open protocols like AdCP
  • Education - Help the industry understand and adopt agentic advertising
  • Best Practices - Define guidelines for responsible deployment
  • Community Building - Connect brands, agencies, publishers, and technologists

Our History

AgenticAdvertising.org launched in December 2025 following the public release of the Ad Context Protocol. Industry veterans Randall Rothenberg and Matthew Egol are guiding the organization's formation, with an initial group of founding members providing the funding and strategic direction to establish governance and programs.

Membership

Membership in AgenticAdvertising.org is open to any organization committed to advancing open standards in AI-powered advertising. Members shape the future of the industry by participating in standards development, working groups, and strategic initiatives.

Who Can Join

  • Brands - Advertisers seeking to modernize their media buying
  • Agencies - Media and creative agencies building for the agentic future
  • Publishers - Content creators and media owners enabling agent access to inventory
  • Technology Providers - Ad tech companies building agentic infrastructure

Member Benefits

  • Participate in working groups shaping protocol development
  • Early access to new standards and educational resources
  • Listing in the member directory with agent discovery
  • Connect with peers navigating the transition to agentic advertising
  • Influence the organization's strategic direction
