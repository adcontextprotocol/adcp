First Annual General Meeting

Our inaugural Annual General Meeting (AGM) is planned for April 2026, where the membership will elect the first full board of directors. All voting members in good standing will be eligible to vote.

Board Structure

The elected board will consist of 40 directors plus the CEO, with equal representation across four member categories. This balanced structure ensures no single sector can dominate the organization's direction.

Member Category Board Seats
Brands (Advertisers) 10
Agencies 10
Publishers 10
Technology Providers 10

Executive Committee

The board will elect an Executive Committee to handle time-sensitive decisions between full board meetings:

  • 3 directors from each voting category (12 total)
  • Plus the CEO

Director Terms

  • Directors serve 3-year terms
  • Terms are staggered so ~1/3 of the board is elected each year
  • Maximum of 2 consecutive terms, then a 1-year break before re-election

Member Categories

The Agentic Advertising Network has four voting member categories, reflecting the major constituencies of the advertising ecosystem. Each category has equal representation on the board regardless of company size.

Brands (Advertisers)

Companies that advertise their products and services. This includes direct-to-consumer brands, enterprise companies, and retail media network operators acting as advertisers.

Agencies

Companies that provide advertising services on behalf of advertisers, including media agencies, creative agencies, and holding company units.

Publishers

Companies that create content and sell advertising inventory, including digital publishers, broadcasters, out-of-home media owners, and sales houses.

Technology Providers

Companies that provide advertising technology infrastructure, including DSPs, SSPs, ad servers, measurement providers, and data platforms.

Note: Organizations that qualify for multiple categories must choose a single category when joining. Category changes require board approval and can only occur once every 24 months.

Transparency Principles

  • Open meetings - Board meetings will have published agendas and minutes
  • Public bylaws - Our governing documents are available to everyone
  • Financial transparency - Annual financial reports will be shared with membership
  • Equal voice - Each member category has equal representation regardless of company size

Delaware Incorporation

The Agentic Advertising Network is incorporated in Delaware, which provides:

  • Flexible governance framework under the Delaware General Corporation Law (DGCL)
  • Customizable member classes, voting rights, and dispute procedures
  • Streamlined formation with fewer state-level approvals
  • Predictable adjudication through the Court of Chancery
  • Lighter ongoing compliance requirements

Get Involved

There are several ways to participate in the governance of the Agentic Advertising Network:

Serve on the Board

Board nominations open before each Annual General Meeting. Directors must be employees of voting member organizations.

Join a Working Group

Working groups shape protocol development and organizational priorities. All members can participate.

Become a Member

Membership is open to organizations in any of our four member categories. Members can vote in elections and shape the organization's direction.
