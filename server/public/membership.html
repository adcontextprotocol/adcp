What Membership Means

Membership in AgenticAdvertising.org means joining a community of forward-thinking organizations and individuals who believe the future of advertising should be built on open standards, not proprietary platforms.

As a member, you're not just subscribing to a service. You're investing in shared infrastructure that benefits the entire advertising ecosystem. Your membership dues fund:

  • Standards Development - Engineering resources to maintain and evolve AdCP and related protocols
  • Education & Advocacy - Programs to help the industry understand and adopt agentic advertising
  • Working Groups - Forums for members to collaborate on specific challenges and opportunities
  • Community Building - Events, resources, and connections that bring the ecosystem together

Governance & Board

AgenticAdvertising.org is committed to transparent, member-driven governance. The organization's direction will be set by a board elected from and by the membership.

Advisory Council

During the formation phase, founding members participate in the Advisory Council - a collaborative group that helps shape early strategy, governance structures, and the member value proposition. The Advisory Council provides input and feedback as we build the organization.

Initial Board Selection

The initial board will be selected in Q1 2026 from among the founding members. This is a unique opportunity to help establish the governance structures and strategic priorities that will guide the organization for years to come.

  • Who can vote: All founding members (corporate and individual) who join before March 31, 2026
  • Who is eligible to serve: Founding members in good standing
  • Board composition: Will represent a balance of buyers (brands/agencies), sellers (publishers), and technology providers
  • Selection process: Details will be announced in January 2026

Recommendation: If you want to participate in the initial board selection process, we encourage you to join as a founding member in Q1 2026 so you can be part of shaping the organization's governance from the start.

Ongoing Governance

After the initial formation period, the board will establish regular governance processes including:

  • Annual board elections with member voting
  • Working group charters and oversight
  • Financial transparency and reporting
  • Standards development governance

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose which membership tier is right for me?
All membership tiers include identical benefits within their category (corporate or individual). Choose the tier that best reflects your organization's size and ability to contribute. We trust members to self-select honestly.
Can I pay monthly instead of annually?
Currently, we only offer annual memberships. This helps us plan and invest in programs that benefit the community. Monthly billing may be available in the future.
What if my organization is a non-profit?
Non-profit organizations typically qualify for lower-tier pricing regardless of budget size. Contact us to discuss your situation and we'll find the right fit.
Do I need to be an AAO member to use AdCP?
No. AdCP is an open protocol that anyone can implement. Membership in AgenticAdvertising.org is about participating in the community that develops and governs these standards, not about access to the technology itself.
How will rates change in 2026?
We expect rates to evolve as the organization grows its programs and services. We may also introduce additional tiers or membership types. Current founding member rates are available through Q1 2026.
