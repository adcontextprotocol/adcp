Why join

AI agents are learning to buy and sell advertising. The standards that govern how they do it are being written right now. The organizations in the room during that process will shape the rules for everyone else.

AgenticAdvertising.org is where that work happens. We're a member organization — brands, agencies, publishers, ad tech companies, and individual practitioners — building the open infrastructure for agentic advertising together.

This is early. There's no legacy to navigate, no entrenched interests to work around. The people who join now are founding members, with real influence over the protocol, the governance, and the direction of the organization.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be a member to use AdCP?
No. AdCP is an open protocol that anyone can implement. Membership is about participating in the community that develops and governs the standard — not about access to the technology itself.
How do I choose the right membership tier?
Explorer ($50/yr) to get fully certified — all three tiers, including Practitioner and Specialist — and see what agentic advertising is about. Professional ($250/yr) if you're a working practitioner who wants full community access and council participation. Builder ($2,500/yr) if you're a growing team that needs API access and multiple seats. Partner ($10,000/yr) if you're an established organization that wants to train your team and participate in governance. Leader ($50,000/yr) for organizations that want to convene councils, shape industry direction, and get first access to marketing opportunities.
Does Explorer unlock the full certification path?
Yes. Every membership tier — including Explorer — unlocks all three certification tiers: Basics, Practitioner, and Specialist. Basics (Tier 1) is free for everyone, even without a membership. Practitioner (Tier 2) and Specialist (Tier 3) require any active membership.
What happens after the founding member window closes?
Pricing will increase and new tier structures will be introduced. Founding members keep their rates as long as they maintain continuous membership. The Advisory Council and board voting rights earned during the founding period are permanent member benefits.
Can I pay monthly instead of annually?
Currently, we only offer annual memberships. This helps us plan and invest in programs that benefit the community. Monthly billing may be available in the future.
What if my organization is a non-profit?
Non-profit organizations typically qualify for lower-tier pricing regardless of budget size. Contact us and we'll find the right fit.
What's the difference between a contributor seat and a community-only seat?
Contributor seats include full community access plus Slack, working groups, industry councils, and product summit. Community-only seats give access to Addie, certification, training, and regional chapters — everything except the active collaboration tools. Use community-only seats for team members who need to learn but don't need to be in Slack or working groups daily.
Can I add more seats later?
Yes. Need more seats than your tier includes? Contact us for a custom package.
What does API access include?
API access includes the AdCP registry, agent validation, brand resolution, and a sandbox for testing your agents against the protocol. You can test agent-to-agent interactions, validate compliance, and integrate AAO data into your products before going live.
I'm a publisher. What does membership do for me?
AdCP gives buy-side agents a standardized way to discover your inventory, respect your pricing, and measure outcomes on your terms. As a member, you shape exactly how that works — from floor price enforcement to brand safety rules that agents must follow. Your team gets certified, your properties get listed in the registry, and you have a seat at the table when sell-side standards are written.
Can my agency partners use our seats?
Yes. Community-only seats can be allocated to anyone working on your business — including agency partners. If your Publicis or GroupM team needs to understand agentic advertising in the context of your brand, they can use your organization's seats to get certified and access Addie.
What's the cancellation policy?
Memberships are annual. You can cancel at any time and your membership will remain active through the end of your paid period. We do not offer prorated refunds for early cancellation.
