Why join

AI agents are learning to buy and sell advertising. The standards that govern how they do it are being written right now. The organizations in the room during that process will shape the rules for everyone else.

AgenticAdvertising.org is where that work happens. We're a member organization — brands, agencies, publishers, ad tech companies, and individual practitioners — building the open infrastructure for agentic advertising together.

This is early. There's no legacy to navigate, no entrenched interests to work around. The people who join now are founding members, with real influence over the protocol, the governance, and the direction of the organization.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be a member to use AdCP?
No. AdCP is an open protocol that anyone can implement. Membership is about participating in the community that develops and governs the standard — not about access to the technology itself.
How do I choose the right membership tier?
If you're a company in the ad ecosystem (brand, agency, publisher, ad tech provider), Voting Membership gives you governance rights. If you're an individual practitioner, Individual Membership gives you education and community access. Associate Membership is for adjacent organizations that want to participate without governance rights.
What happens after the founding member window closes?
Pricing will increase and new tier structures will be introduced. Founding members keep their rates as long as they maintain continuous membership. The Advisory Council and board voting rights earned during the founding period are permanent member benefits.
Can I pay monthly instead of annually?
Currently, we only offer annual memberships. This helps us plan and invest in programs that benefit the community. Monthly billing may be available in the future.
What if my organization is a non-profit?
Non-profit organizations typically qualify for lower-tier pricing regardless of budget size. Contact us and we'll find the right fit.
How many people from my organization can participate?
Company memberships (Voting Member) cover your entire organization. Every team member can participate in working groups, education programs, and community events. The governance vote is one vote per member organization.
