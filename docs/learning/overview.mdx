# AdCP certification program

The AdCP certification program teaches you agentic advertising through interactive, AI-guided modules. You'll learn by doing — discussing concepts with [Addie](https://agenticadvertising.org/addie), our AI teaching assistant, and working with live sandbox agents.

## Three-tier credential model

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Level 1 — AdCP basics" icon="circle-check">
    **Free and open to everyone.** Complete two foundation modules to demonstrate you understand what agentic advertising is and how AdCP works.

    Modules: [A1](/docs/learning/foundations/a1-agentic-advertising), [A2](/docs/learning/foundations/a2-protocol-architecture)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Level 2 — AdCP practitioner" icon="medal">
    **Foundations plus a specialization.** Complete all three foundation modules and one specialization track (publisher, buyer, or platform) with hands-on exercises.

    Modules: [A1](/docs/learning/foundations/a1-agentic-advertising)–[A3](/docs/learning/foundations/a3-ecosystem-governance) + one track
  </Card>
  <Card title="Level 3 — AdCP specialist" icon="trophy">
    **Protocol mastery.** Pass a capstone combining hands-on lab and adaptive exam in a specific protocol area: media buy, creative, signals, or governance.

    Requires: Practitioner + capstone
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

Each module follows the same pattern:

1. **Read** — Review the reading list on this page to build context
2. **Learn** — Start the module with Addie for interactive, Socratic-style teaching
3. **Practice** — Work through exercises against live sandbox agents
4. **Assess** — Addie evaluates your understanding and scores your work

There are no multiple-choice tests. Addie assesses your understanding through conversation — can you explain concepts clearly? Can you apply them to real scenarios? Can you use the protocol tools correctly?

## Learning paths

### Foundations (required for all credentials)

Everyone starts here. These modules are prerequisites for everything else.

| Module | Topic | Duration | Free? |
|--------|-------|----------|-------|
| [A1](/docs/learning/foundations/a1-agentic-advertising) | Agentic advertising concepts | 12 min | Yes |
| [A2](/docs/learning/foundations/a2-protocol-architecture) | Protocol architecture | 15 min | Yes |
| [A3](/docs/learning/foundations/a3-ecosystem-governance) | Ecosystem and governance | 18 min | Members |

### Specialization tracks (choose your path)

After foundations, choose the track that matches your role. Complete any one track to earn the Practitioner credential.

| Track | For | Modules | Duration |
|-------|-----|---------|----------|
| [Publisher / seller](/docs/learning/tracks/publisher) | Publishers, SSPs, supply-side platforms | B1–B3 | ~45 min |
| [Buyer / brand](/docs/learning/tracks/buyer) | Brands, agencies, DSPs | C1–C3 | ~42 min |
| [Platform / intermediary](/docs/learning/tracks/platform) | Ad tech platforms, exchanges, data companies | D1–D3 | ~39 min |

### Specialist capstones (protocol mastery)

Each capstone combines a hands-on lab with an adaptive exam in a specific protocol area. Requires the Practitioner credential.

| Capstone | Protocol area | Duration |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| [Media buy](/docs/learning/specialist/media-buy) | Transactions, pricing, multi-agent orchestration | 30 min |
| [Creative](/docs/learning/specialist/creative) | Asset workflows, format compliance, cross-platform adaptation | 30 min |
| [Signals](/docs/learning/specialist/signals) | Measurement, attribution, optimization loops | 30 min |
| [Governance](/docs/learning/specialist/governance) | Brand safety, supply chain compliance, content standards | 30 min |
