# S3: Signals and audiences

<Info>
**Members only** — Requires Practitioner credential. ~45 minutes with Addie. Combines hands-on lab and adaptive exam.
</Info>

This specialist module tests your mastery of the signals protocol. You'll work with sandbox agents to discover available measurement signals, activate them on campaigns, manage audience segments, configure event tracking, and design optimization loops. Addie evaluates both your hands-on work and your conceptual understanding.

Passing earns the **AdCP specialist — Signals** credential.

## What you'll demonstrate

- Discover and evaluate available measurement signals across publishers
- Activate appropriate signals for different campaign objectives
- Manage audience activation with `sync_audiences` including privacy considerations
- Configure event tracking with `sync_event_sources` and `log_event`
- Design optimization loops using signals and delivery data
- Reason about attribution models, data quality, and measurement methodology

## Lab exercises

1. **Signal discovery** — Query sandbox agents for available signals, compare measurement capabilities
2. **Signal activation** — Activate viewability and conversion signals on a sandbox campaign
3. **Audience management** — Sync audience segments and manage privacy-compliant activation
4. **Optimization loop design** — Given delivery data and signals, propose and execute campaign optimizations
