# S5: Generative advertising

<Info>
**Members only** — Requires Practitioner credential. ~45 minutes with Addie. Combines hands-on lab and adaptive exam.
</Info>

This specialist module covers the three pillars of generative advertising: AI-generated creative from brand assets and catalogs, AI media buying where data flows into the platform instead of bid requests flowing out, and Sponsored Intelligence where brands engage users in multi-turn conversations. Addie evaluates both your hands-on work and your understanding of when and why generative advertising fits.

Passing earns the **AdCP specialist — Generative advertising** credential.

## What you'll demonstrate

- Generate creative from brand assets, catalog data, and natural language briefs
- Execute AI media campaigns using the reversed data flow paradigm
- Manage Sponsored Intelligence sessions for conversational brand experiences
- Explain account models for walled gardens vs agent-trusted networks
- Reason about when generative advertising fits vs traditional approaches

## Lab exercises

During the module, Addie will guide you through hands-on exercises:

1. **Generative creative** — Discover formats, generate creative from brand assets and a brief, evaluate output quality
2. **AI media campaign** — Push catalogs to a platform, discover `ai_media` products, create a media buy with optimization goals
3. **SI session** — Initiate a session, exchange messages with offering integration, observe commerce handoff
4. **Strategic evaluation** — Compare generative advertising vs traditional approaches for different scenarios
