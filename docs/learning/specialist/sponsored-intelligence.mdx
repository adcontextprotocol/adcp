# S5: Sponsored Intelligence

<Info>
**Members only** — Requires Practitioner credential. ~45 minutes with Addie. Combines hands-on lab and adaptive exam.
</Info>

This specialist module tests your mastery of the Sponsored Intelligence protocol. You'll work with sandbox agents to manage the full session lifecycle: discovering offerings, initiating sessions, exchanging messages, and terminating sessions. Addie evaluates both your hands-on work and your conceptual understanding.

Passing earns the **AdCP specialist — Sponsored Intelligence** credential.

## What you'll demonstrate

- Discover available SI offerings and evaluate their suitability
- Manage the full session lifecycle: initiate, message, terminate
- Design conversational brand experiences with transparency and user control
- Reason about cost-per-conversation economics and performance measurement
- Understand A2UI components for rich conversational interfaces

## Lab exercises

1. **Offering discovery** — Query sandbox agents for SI offerings, evaluate pricing and targeting
2. **Session lifecycle** — Initiate a session, exchange messages, observe the conversation flow
3. **Experience design** — Design a conversational brand experience with appropriate transparency
4. **Economics analysis** — Analyze cost-per-conversation vs traditional CPM for different scenarios
