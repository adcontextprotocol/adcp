# S4: Governance and brand safety

<Info>
**Members only** — Requires Practitioner credential. ~45 minutes with Addie. Combines hands-on lab and adaptive exam.
</Info>

This specialist module tests your mastery of the governance protocol. You'll work with sandbox agents to manage the full CRUD lifecycle of content standards and property lists, calibrate content, validate delivery, and verify supply chain compliance. Addie evaluates both your hands-on work and your conceptual understanding.

Passing earns the **AdCP specialist — Governance** credential.

## What you'll demonstrate

- Create, update, list, and delete content standards
- Create, update, list, and delete property lists
- Calibrate content against standards and interpret results
- Verify supply chain compliance and delivery validation
- Reason about brand safety, transparency, and regulatory requirements

## Lab exercises

1. **Content standards lifecycle** — Create, update, and manage content standards for a fictional brand
2. **Property list management** — Create inclusion and exclusion lists for supply path control
3. **Content calibration** — Calibrate sample content against your standards and interpret results
4. **Compliance verification** — Verify that sandbox campaign deliveries meet governance requirements
