# Specialist capstone — Governance (E4)

<Info>
**Members only** — Requires Practitioner credential. ~30 minutes with Addie. Combines hands-on lab and adaptive exam.
</Info>

This capstone tests your mastery of the governance protocol. You'll work with sandbox agents to create property lists, define content standards, calibrate content against brand requirements, and verify supply chain compliance. Addie evaluates both your hands-on work and your conceptual understanding.

Passing earns the **AdCP specialist — Governance** credential.

## What you'll demonstrate

- Create and manage property lists for supply path control
- Define content standards and calibrate content against them
- Verify supply chain compliance using governance tools
- Reason about brand safety, transparency, and regulatory requirements

## Lab exercises

1. **Property list management** — Create inclusion and exclusion lists for supply path control
2. **Content standards definition** — Define content standards for a fictional brand with specific requirements
3. **Content calibration** — Calibrate sample content against your standards and interpret results
4. **Compliance verification** — Verify that sandbox campaign deliveries meet governance requirements
