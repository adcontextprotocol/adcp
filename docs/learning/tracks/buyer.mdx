# Buyer / brand track (C1–C3)

<Info>
**Members only** — Requires A3 completion. Three modules, ~42 minutes total.
</Info>

This track teaches you the demand side of AdCP. You'll learn how buyer agents orchestrate across multiple sellers, how brand identity and compliance protocols work, and how creative workflows and Sponsored Intelligence fit together.

Completing this track (plus A1–A3) earns the **AdCP practitioner** credential.

---
