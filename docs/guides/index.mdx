## What each industry guide covers

Every industry guide follows the same structure:

- **Concept mapping** — How industry terminology maps to AdCP fields and schemas
- **Product spectrum** — The range of media products available in that vertical, with JSON examples
- **End-to-end workflow** — From product discovery through delivery reporting
- **Measurement** — Attribution models and KPIs specific to the vertical
- **Best practices** — Guidance for both buyers and sellers
